Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Verified Global E-Transport

Booking & Payment

Booking is simple! Just visit our homepage, select your departure and destination cities, choose your travel date, and click "Search Rides". You'll see available buses with real-time seat availability. Select your preferred time, choose your seat, and complete the payment.

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Yes! You can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 2 hours of departure are subject to a 20% cancellation fee. To cancel, go to "My Bookings" in your account.

Travel & Safety

Yes! Our entire fleet consists of 100% electric vehicles. We're committed to zero-emission transportation and reducing Ghana's carbon footprint. All our buses are modern, air-conditioned, and equipped with the latest safety features.

After booking, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. Click the link or log into your account to see your bus's live location on the map, estimated arrival time, and driver details.

All our drivers undergo thorough background checks and regular training. Buses are equipped with GPS tracking, emergency buttons, first aid kits, and fire extinguishers. We also have 24/7 monitoring and customer support.

Sustainability

On average, a passenger on a Verified Global E-Transport electric bus saves approximately 0.8 kg of CO₂ per 100 km compared to traditional diesel buses. Use our Carbon Footprint Calculator to see your personal impact!

We have charging stations at all our major terminals and depots across Ghana. Our charging infrastructure uses renewable energy sources wherever possible, further reducing our environmental impact.
