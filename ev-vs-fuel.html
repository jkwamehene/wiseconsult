Why Electric is Better

Lower Operating Costs

Electric vehicles have 60-70% lower energy costs compared to fuel vehicles, with additional savings on maintenance.

Less Maintenance

EVs have fewer moving parts, no oil changes, and reduced brake wear, cutting maintenance costs by up to 50%.

Stable Energy Prices

Electricity prices are more stable than fuel prices, providing predictable operating costs for better budgeting.
